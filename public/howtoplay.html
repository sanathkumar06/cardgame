• How to play

First Off, There are two types of Cards

CARD 1 - CATEGORY

BUSINESS STYLES, REVENUE, MODELS AND MOBILE APPS

CARD 2 - DEMOGRAPHIC

EACH CARD HAS A UNIQUE DEMOGRAPHIC


  • Each player will have a Business Model and Target Audience

  • Each player will have time limit to decide his idea

  • When the player is ready, they can start pitching their ideas

  • The mentor can choose the best idea from the players

  • After the mentor is convinced, They can move on to the new game
